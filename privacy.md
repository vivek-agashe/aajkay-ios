# Privacy Policy for "Aaj Kay?"

**Last Updated: March 22, 2026**

## Introduction

This Privacy Policy describes how "Aaj Kay?" ("we," "our," or "the app") handles your information. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

## Information We Collect

### Information You Provide

When you use "Aaj Kay?", you may provide the following information:

- **User Profile Information**: Your display name, family name (if using family mode), and user preferences
- **Recipe Data**: Recipe titles, ingredients, instructions, categories, and other recipe-related content you create
- **User-Generated Content**: Notes, custom ingredient lists, and other content you add to recipes

### Automatically Collected Information

"Aaj Kay?" does **NOT** collect any information automatically. We do not use analytics, tracking, cookies, or any other automatic data collection methods.

## How We Store Your Information

### Local Storage Only

All data you create in "Aaj Kay?" is stored **exclusively on your device** using Apple's SwiftData framework. This includes:

- All recipes you create or import
- Recipe categories and organization
- User profile information
- App preferences and settings
- Favorite recipes and other personalization

**We do not have access to your data.** Your information never leaves your device except when you explicitly choose to export and share recipes.

### iCloud Storage

"Aaj Kay?" does **NOT** automatically sync your data to iCloud or any cloud service. Your data remains entirely on your local device.

## How We Use Your Information

Your information is used solely for the following purposes:

- **App Functionality**: To provide recipe management features within the app
- **Personalization**: To display your name and organize your content according to your preferences
- **Family Sharing**: To identify recipe authors when you manually share recipes with family members via file export

## Data Sharing and Disclosure

### Manual Recipe Sharing

You may choose to export and share recipes with others. When you do this:

- Recipe data is exported to a file that you control
- Exported recipes include your display name (or family name if in family mode) to identify the recipe author
- You decide who receives the exported file
- We have no access to or control over exported files once you share them

### No Third-Party Sharing

We do **NOT**:

- Share your data with third parties
- Sell your data
- Use your data for advertising
- Send your data to external servers
- Use analytics or tracking services

## Data Security

Your data security is important to us:

- All data is stored locally on your device using Apple's secure SwiftData framework
- We rely on your device's built-in security features (passcode, Face ID, Touch ID, etc.)
- No data transmission to external servers means no risk of data breaches from our end
- Only you have access to your data through your device

## Your Rights and Choices

You have complete control over your data:

- **Access**: You can view all your data within the app at any time
- **Modification**: You can edit or update any information you've entered
- **Deletion**: You can delete individual recipes, categories, or all data by deleting the app from your device
- **Export**: You can export your recipes at any time to back them up or share them

## Children's Privacy

"Aaj Kay?" does not knowingly collect information from children under 13. Since all data is stored locally and we have no access to it, we cannot determine the age of our users. Parents and guardians should supervise their children's use of the app.

## Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. When we make changes:

- We will update the "Last Updated" date at the top of this policy
- If we make material changes, we will notify you through the app or app store listing
- Your continued use of the app after changes constitutes acceptance of the updated policy

## Data Retention

Your data remains on your device for as long as you keep the app installed. To delete all data:

1. Delete all content within the app, or
2. Delete the app from your device

Once deleted from your device, your data cannot be recovered (unless you have created your own backups via export).

## International Users

"Aaj Kay?" stores all data locally on your device, regardless of your location. We do not transfer data internationally because we do not collect or transmit your data.
